Highlights

  • Imported to the U.S. by Europa International of Sante Fe, New Mexico in 2002 by the prior owner.
  • Refinished in green over black leather and is powered by a 5.0-liter V8 paired with a five-speed automatic transmission, a two-speed transfer case, and three locking differentials
  • Equipment includes a power-operated black soft top, aftermarket 20″ wheels, a Brabus-branded exhaust muffler, a brush guard, fog lights, running boards, rear parking sensors, heated front and rear seats, air conditioning, a Bluetooth-capable Alpine CD stereo, and a McIntosh amplifier.
  • Partial service records, owner’s manuals, the removed stock wheels, switchblade key, valet key, and a soft top boot included in sale.
  • Aftermarket 20″ wheels are finished in black and wrapped in Pirelli Scorpion Zero tires sized 295/45. The truck is equipped with four-wheel disc brakes, and the brake fluid was recently changed.

Amenities include power-adjustable and heated front seats, heated rear seats, cruise control, air conditioning, dual front airbags, a McIntosh rear-mounted amplifier, and a Bluetooth-capable Alpine CD stereo with auxiliary inputs.
